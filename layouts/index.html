The Waccamaw Indian People have kept a presence online since 2001. This is an archive of waccamaw.org through the years — every incarnation of the tribe's website we can recover — alongside exhibits on the People, our history, and the leadership of Chief Hatcher.

Built in the spirit of preservation: the public pages show what the tribe shared with the world, while a private research archive gathers the fuller record for study and future publication. Nothing sacred or restricted is published here.
