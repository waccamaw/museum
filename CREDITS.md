# Credits

This archive is built on other people's open work:

- **[The Internet Archive / Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/)** — the
  primary source for recovering old incarnations of waccamaw.org. The snapshots
  preserved here are pulled from their captures; we mirror the bytes locally so the
  museum does not depend on their servers at runtime.
- **[Ruffle](https://ruffle.rs/)** — the open-source Flash (.swf) emulator that
  lets period Flash content play again in a modern browser.
- **[Hugo](https://gohugo.io/)** — the static-site generator that builds the museum.

The photographs, words, and design of the Waccamaw Indian People's sites belong to
the tribe and its members. They are gathered here for preservation and study.
